About Me

I’m a passionate front-end developer with a strong focus on building clean, responsive, and user-friendly websites using HTML and CSS. I enjoy turning design ideas into real, working websites that look great on all devices. My coding journey started with curiosity about how websites work, and it quickly grew into a love for creating digital experiences. I’m currently improving my JavaScript skills to add interactivity and functionality to my projects. My long-term goal is to become a full-stack web developer and work on projects that make an impact. I believe in continuous learning, simplicity in design, and writing code that not only works but is easy to read and maintain.

As a frontend developer, I focus on transforming design ideas into interactive and responsive web experiences. I work with tools like HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to bring life to digital interfaces. My goal is to make every section of a website not just look good, but also feel good to use. I pay attention to spacing, color, and smooth layouts to ensure that everything fits together perfectly — just like how designs do in Figma. I’m also passionate about learning modern frontend frameworks and improving my workflow. I believe every project is a chance to grow and get better at turning creativity into clean, working code that people can actually use.
